EP 17.1 - Illusion

(for new character)
Require Lv. 110

New World access quest

  1. Go to the Eden Group Headquarters and then talk with the Cat Hand agent, who is situated near the desk on the first floor (ground floor).
    • Choose "Why don't you collect it yourself?", and then "I'll help the merchants".
  2. Pay the one-time fee of 50,000 zeny, and he will then teleport you to the quarantined area of the Sograt Desert.
  3. Walk east and then talk with the cat (moc_fild20 368, 197)Copied!
    • Choose "Let's do this!", and he will then teleport you to the Dimensional Gorge.
  4. Walk north and then talk with the Cat Hand agent.
    • Choose "To the other world!", and he will then teleport you to the New World.


EP 16.1 - The Royal Banquet

Learning About the Families

  1. Speak with the Royal Errand Boy (prontera 161, 179)Copied! in Prontera to start the quest. He will give you a Banquet Invitation .
  2. Go in the Prontera Castle (@go woe, then walk north). Upon entering the castle, Chamberlain Bell will ask to follow him.
  3. Walk towards the left wing and enter the portal. Keep going forward to enter your room and Chamberlain Bell will be waiting for you inside.
  4. Speak with Chamberlain Bell, he asks you meet with all the members of the other families. You can always come back to him to know which family you haven't talked to yet.
    1. The Heine (left wing, bottom room):
      Walk near the table and a cutscene will start. Speak with Kronecker G. Heine afterwards.
    2. The Nerius (left wing, middle room):
      A cutscene will trigger when you leave the Heine room. Enter the Nerius room and speak with Spica Nerius.
    3. The Walthers (left wing, top room):
      Speak with Ian Walther and then Isenhonor Walther.
      You can now start The Walthers storyline.
    4. The Wigners (right wing, bottom room):
      Speak with Levuiere Wigner and then Jurgen Wigner.
      You can now start The Wigners storyline.
    5. The Richards (right wing, middle room):
      Speak with Butler for the Richards and then Po Richard right next to him.
      You can speak with Po Richard again to unlock the The Royal Richard daily quest.
    6. The Geoborgs (right wing, middle room):
      Enter the Geoborgs's room and a scene will trigger. Speak with the Queen and select Ask about the Geoborg family.
      You can now start The Geoborgs storyline.
    7. The Roegenburg (right wing, top room):
      Speak with Maximilian Roegenburg and then Wolf Roegenburg.
      You can now start The Roegenburgs storyline.
  5. Once you've visited all the families, come back to your room and talk with Chamberlain Bell. He will reward you with 10 Honor Token and some experience.
  6. You can continue the quest with the Ritual of Blessing.


Ritual of Blessing

  1. Bell has asked you to participate in the event and meet with Spica in the Banquet Hall. On your way there (prt_cas 174, 163)Copied!, you will trigger a cutscene.
  2. Go find Spica Nerius in the Banquet Hall and she'll ask you to follow Nyhill since he was acting strange.
  3. Leave the Banquet Hall and on your way back to your room, you will trigger another cutscene.
  4. Go in the room right below the cutscene to meet with Skia Nerius and Nyhill.
  5. Speak with Nyhill in the room. You'll have to go back to your room and speak with Chamberlain Bell afterwards.
  6. Talk with Chamberlain Bell and Nillem will appear next to you. After the cutscene, you'll be warped to the Past Memories of the Royal Family.
  7. While in a party, speak with Mage Nillem and choose Generate instance to attend the Ritual of Blessing. Then talk to the Ritual of Blessing warper.
    1. Speak with Skia at the entrance to start the ritual.
    2. Take your position in the empty space, on the right side.
    3. Enjoy the cutscene...! Walk up a bit to watch the important part.
    4. The Ritual of Blessing is under attack by intruders, fight them off.
    5. Speak with Kronecker G. Heine to finish up the quest and then leave the room by walking down towards the exit.
  8. Back in the Past Memories of the Royal Family, you'll see all the family members waiting for you.
  9. Speak with Ian Walther to know more about what's going on.
  10. After the members leave, talk to Mage Nillem. He wants you to take a look at the royal document Kronecker was looking at.
  11. Speak with Lindt the Librarian and then talk with Mage Nillem again.
  12. Leave the Past Memories of the Royal Family by speaking with the Guardian Knight on the left side. Select Get out now and you'll be warped into the prison.
  13. Talk to Overwatcher Terketh to get you out of the cell.
    You can talk with him again to be warped back inside the Past Memories of the Royal Family at any moment.
  14. Go back to Chamberlain Bell in your room and tell him what happened.
  15. You'll be invited to the Nerius family, go to their room and speak with 'Spica Nerius'.
  16. Go visit the Heine in the room below. Speak to Kronocker G. Heine and listen to what he has to say. You'll be rewarded with 20 Honor Token and some experience.
  17. You can continue the quest with Room of Consciousness.


EP 16.2 - Terra Gloria

Search for Terra Gloria

  1. Go in the Prontera Castle and head to your room (prt_cas_q 29, 28)Copied!. Nihil has been waiting for you and he asks you to wait for Spica.
  2. Once she arrives, speak with Spica. You learn that the chamberlain who gave the drink to Nihil was from the Schwarzwald Republic.
  3. Speak with Nihil again, he'll set up a meeting with Crux.
  4. Go see Crux in the Lighthalzen Airport (lhz_airport 189, 39)Copied!. He suspects that the culprit's name is most likely Bruno.
  5. Search the north side of Lightalzen, near (lighthalzen 186, 293)Copied!.
  6. Investigate the slums and speak with Bruno again (lighthalzen 309, 263)Copied!. Crux apprehends Bruno and will interrogate him later.
  7. Head back to your room in the Prontera Castle (prt_cas_q 29, 28)Copied! and talk to Nihil.
  8. Go to Yuno and enter the Yuno Airport. Crux will be waiting for you there. (y_airport 168, 41)Copied!.
  9. Go to the Lighthalzen Hotel (lhz_in02 238, 171)Copied! and speak with Philofontes. You'll have to join the Rebellion Empire and defeat Luke.
  10. Go to Einbroch and head to the Train Station (einbroch 236, 268)Copied!.
  11. Talk to the Unsual Person (Lazy) right next to you, it's the agent of the Secret Wings.
  12. Lazy doesn't want to pay the 200z train fee, so follow him near the sewer instead (einbroch 270, 268)Copied!.
  13. Click the Very Small Hole to be transported to Einbech.
  14. Speak with Lazy in Einbech (einbech 46, 208)Copied!. Follow his traces.
  15. Lazy will be waiting for you near (einbech 76, 131)Copied!, talk to him. You find a body and decide to carry him along to the Rebellion camp.
  16. Speak with Lazy again (einbech 216, 82)Copied! at the entrance of Clana Nemieri. Enter the portal afterwards.
  17. Speak with Lazy inside Clana Nemieri (rebel_in 21, 45)Copied! and a cutscene will trigger.
  18. Walk accross the corridor and speak with Lazy again (rebel_in 85, 61)Copied!. You'll trigger another cutscene and the negotations will begin.
  19. You'll have to start by speaking with Elena Bolkova (rebel_in 69, 78)Copied!. She will challenge you and you must defeat her.
  20. Speak with the Rebellion Leader on the second floor (rebel_in 122, 71)Copied!. He won't accept to cooperate with you, so you'll have to try to convince Dien.
  21. Enter the portal at (rebel_in 123, 41)Copied! and a cutscene will trigger. You'll be warped into Dien's room.
  22. Walk to close Dien to trigger the next cutscene. They will need some time to think about your proposal.
  23. Speak with Dien again.
  24. Speak with Special Force commander (rebel_in 209, 84)Copied!, she is at the top middle room. She agrees with Dien.
  25. Go back to the Rebellion Leader (rebel_in 122, 71)Copied! and speak with him.
  26. Talk to Rebellion Guard leader who appeared next to him.
  27. Speak with the Rebellion Leader again.
  28. Speak with Dien who just showed up.
  29. Speak with the Rebellion Leader and you will get 10 Schwartz's Honor Token .
    You have now unlocked the Dien's Party quest.
  30. Return downstairs to Lazy (rebel_in 86, 61)Copied! and inform him about your success for the elders' cooperation. You will get another 10 Schwartz's Honor Token along with some experience.
    You have now unlocked the Infiltrate Heart Hunter Base quest.
    You have now unlocked the Rachel's Revolver quest.
    You have now unlocked the Lebedev Twins quest.
    You have now unlocked the Teleportation Prohibition quest.
    You have now unlocked the Leaflets Delivery daily quest.
    You have now unlocked the Luke's Requests daily quest.
    You have now unlocked the Finding Charity daily quest.
    You have now unlocked the Flower Girl daily quest.
    You have now unlocked the Elena's Challenge daily quest.
    You have now unlocked the Refreshments for the Elders daily quest.
    You have now unlocked the Weapon Grudges daily quest.


Infiltrate Heart Hunter Base

  1. Speak with the Rebellion Squadder (rebel_in 65, 46)Copied!, Tess comes up and wants you to come to the Einbroch Hotel.
  2. To go to the Enbroch hotel (einbroch 255, 199)Copied!, and then go to second floor simply walk to the left. The meeting is at (ein_in01 274, 243)Copied!. Speak with Nihil inside the hotel room.
  3. The operation base camp is North of Einbroch, at the northeast corner of the map (ein_fild04 281, 333)Copied!. Speak with Julian and he tell him you're ready. You'll need to make a party for the Heart Hunter War Base 1 instance.
  4. Talk to Julian to reserve the instance, and then Striker Unit Commander to enter.

Heart Hunter War Base 1

  1. Walk near Julian on your right to trigger a cutscene.
  2. Defeat the first wave of Heart Hunters on your right. Once you're done, Julian will reappear near the barricades (if you walk too close to the barricade you'll lose HP, you can't die, but be careful!).
  3. Walk near Julian to trigger the next cutscene. You'll have to find explosives.
  4. The explosives are north of the entrance. Pick up the gunpowder that drops.
  5. You'll have to defeat another wave of Heart Hunters. Speak with Julian and give him the gunpowder to blow up the first barricade.
  6. Keep walking on the right and you'll find Julian again. Walk near him to trigger another cutscene.
  7. Walk north of the area and you'll find a Suspicious Instrument. Push the button and defeat the new wave of Heart Hunters.
  8. Walk near Julian again and talk to him to trigger a cutscene. Go through the wall.
  9. Keep walking to the right until you find Julian again, walk near him to trigger a cutscene.
  10. Go back near the previous wall and defeat the intruders. A new Pile of Gunpowder will appear, click on it and pick up the items.
  11. Go back to Julian (on the right side) and give him the gunpowder.
  12. Walk to the right once more and go near Julian. A cutscene will trigger.
  13. Heart Hunter Ebel will appear, you do not have to kill her just run away until she die. Wait for the animation to finish and then watch the cutscene.
  14. Speak with Julian to get out of the instance and you will receive 10 Schwartz's Honor Token .
    You have unlocked the Heart Hunter War Base daily instance.

Continue with Infiltrate Heart Hunter Base

  1. Speak with Rookie at the operation base camp to get warped inside the Heart Hunter base.
    You have now unlocked the Chimera Research daily quest.
  2. Go in the portal right up to you, to get inside the Werner Laboratory. Then walk towards Rookie near (slabw01 236, 91)Copied! and speak with him.
  3. Talk to him again to create the Werner Laboratory instance. Talk to the portal that appeared to go inside.

Werner Laboratory

  1. Speak with Eisen Werner at the entrance.
  2. Move forward and speak with him again at the end of the corridor.
  3. You'll have to active the Security Devices on each side of the room. If you pick the wrong answer, you'll have to start over with both devices.
    Trick: just spam the first option, red is more likely to be the answer.
  4. Enter the portal to go to the next area.
  5. Move up and speak with Eisen again. You'll have to active the two security devices on the sides once more.
  6. Enter the portal to go to the next area.
  7. Speak with Eisen again and you'll be teleported inside the core of the lab.
  8. Speak with Eisen again and watch the cutscene. On your first time, you will be teleported outside of the instance. You will also get 1 Small Leather Bag with 1 Blessing Star .
    You have now unlocked the Returning the Relics quest.


Continue with Infiltrate Heart Hunter Base

  1. With the Blessing Star in hand, go back to the Hotel Room in Einbroch (ein_in01 274, 243)Copied!.
  2. Speak with Nihil and listen to the story. You'll be teleported to Prontera. Go back to the Prontera Castle inside your room.
  3. Speak with Skia Nerius. You will be rewarded with 10 Honor Tokens .
    You have now unlocked the Pet Child quest.
    You have now unlocked the YSF01 quest.
  4. Speak with Rookie (ein_fild04 284, 332)Copied! at the operation base camp to get warped inside the Heart Hunter base.
  5. Speak with Philofontes at the Heart Hunter base (que_swat 145, 57)Copied!. You will be rewarded with 10 Schwartz's Honor Token .
    You have now completed the Terra Gloria main quest!
